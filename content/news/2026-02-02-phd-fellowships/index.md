We are pleased to announce that Rubén Montagud, a PhD student in our group,
and Luis Orta, a PhD student in [TunonLab](https://www.tunonlab.com/), who
both joined their respective groups last October, have officially started their
funded PhD periods.

Rubén has been awarded a GVA ACIF fellowship by the Generalitat Valenciana,
while Luis receives a FPI fellowship granted by the Ministerio de Ciencia,
Innovación y Universidades.

We congratulate both of them and wish them great success in this exciting stage
of their doctoral research.
